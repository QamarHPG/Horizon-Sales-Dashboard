Re-engagement signal (multi-open leads)
Now follows the date range and campaign dropdowns above. High repeat-open with no reply often means real interest without a strong enough reason to respond yet — worth a direct, personal follow-up outside the sequence. Hot leads are those whose opens exceed the number of emails they've received (and no reply) — they came back to re-read at least one email, rather than just opening each email once.

Note on date range: Instantly's lead data only stores each lead's total open count and the timestamp of their most recent open — not a log of every open event. So "Last 7 days" here means "last opened within the past 7 days," using their lifetime open count — it's a recency filter, not a true count of opens that happened within that window. Lifetime and campaign filtering are both exact.
